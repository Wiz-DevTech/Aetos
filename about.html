Our Mission

Aetos Drone Dynamics exists to accelerate the safe, effective deployment of unmanned systems across defense, public safety, and commercial sectors through comprehensive NDAA-compliant training and testing solutions.

Founded by former military UAV operators, we bridge the gap between emerging drone technology and operational readiness. Our Austin-based facility combines cutting-edge simulation technology with real-world training environments to create the most comprehensive drone education ecosystem in the United States.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of security, compliance, and operational excellence in all our programs.

Our History

Key milestones in our journey

Company Founded

June 2018

November 2020
